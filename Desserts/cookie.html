Ingredients:

  • 227 Butter
  • 396g Bittersweet Chocoalte/Chocolate Chips
  • 232g Granulated Sugar
  • 213g Light Brown Sugar
  • 410g Plain Flour
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 tsp Bicarbonate of Soda
  • 0.5 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 Large Eggs

Instructions:

  1. Cream together butter and sugars in stand mixer
  2. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract
  3. Add all dry ingredients and mix till combined
  4. Add chocolate chips and mix till combined
  5. Add salt and flour to melted chocoalte butter mix
  6. Use spoon or ice cream scoop to sererate into even balls
  7. Let balls set in fridge before moving into resealable bags
  8. Bake in oven at 190°c for 12-15 mins or until top is browning
  9. Serve and eat whilst warm
