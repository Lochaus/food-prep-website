Pizza

Ingredients:

Pizza Dough Ingredients:

  • 530ml Water
  • 12g Sugar
  • 9g (1 tbsp) Dry Yeast
  • 27g (2 tsp) Olive Oil
  • 18g (1 tbsp) Salt
  • 600g Bread Flour
  • 100-300g Extra Bread Flour

Toppings:

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pizza Sauce

Optional Toppings:

  • Pork Crackling Joint
  • Salt
  • Vegtable Oil

Instructions:

  1. Cover the surface of the fat with salt and leave overnight in the fridge to absorb plenty of moisture
  2. Preheat oven to 230°
  3. Brush off all old salt
  4. Pat the skin dry with a paper towel then brush on vegetable oil onto the surface
  5. Season all sides of the pork with a bit of salt then place in the oven
  6. Bake for 30 minutes at 230°c then turn the temperature down to 180 and bake till the internal temperatrue reaches 65°c
  7. Let the pork rest for 15 minutes before carving
