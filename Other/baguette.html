Baguette's

Ingredients:

  • 1000g Strong Bread Flour
  • 10g Dried Yeast
  • 700g Water
  • 20g Sea Salt

Instructions:

  1. Bloom the yeast in the water to make sure it is alive
  2. Add the yeast, water and flour to a stand mixer and mix on low speed for 4 minutes
  3. After minutes turn the speed up slightly and slowly add the salt
  4. Turn dough out onto table and form into a tight ball before placing in flowered bowl to rise
  5. Allow to rise for an hour then place in the fridge overnight
  6. Seperate dough into 6-12 portions
  7. Form into baguette shapes then place on flowered kitchen towel
  8. Heat oven to 230°c
  9. Add 150ml of boiling water to oven then place a baguettte in and cook till golden brown
  10. Repeat for all baguettes
